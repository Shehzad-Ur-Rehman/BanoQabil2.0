Traditional foods, including Biryani and Karahi, Dhaga Kabab, Fry Kabab, Nihari, Haleem, and fried finger fish, are popular items throughout the eateries located on Burns Road. Kheer, Rabri, Ras Malai, Ras Gulay, Lassi, and Dahi Barha are some other famous sweets and savories offered here.
